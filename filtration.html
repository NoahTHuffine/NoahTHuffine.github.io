Types of Filtration

  • Mechanical filtration removes debris
  • Biological filtration supports bacteria
  • Chemical filtration removes dissolved compounds

Filter Types

  • Canister Filter
  • Hang-on-Back Filter
  • Sponge Filter
  • Internal Filter

Pros and Cons

Canister filters provide strong filtration but are expensive. Hang-on-back filters are simple and affordable. Sponge filters are gentle and great for breeding tanks.

Filter Maintenance

Filter media should be cleaned with aquarium water to preserve beneficial bacteria.
