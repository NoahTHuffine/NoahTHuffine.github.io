Aquarium Filtration

Types of Filtration

  • Mechanical filtration removes debris
  • Biological filtration houses beneficial bacteria
  • Chemical filtration removes dissolved chemicals

Filter Types

  • Canister filters
  • Hang-on-back filters
  • Sponge filters
  • Internal filters

Maintenance

Filter media should be rinsed in aquarium water to protect beneficial bacteria.
