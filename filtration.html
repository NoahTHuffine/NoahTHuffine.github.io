Aquarium Filtration

Types of Filtration

  • Mechanical filtration removes debris
  • Biological filtration supports beneficial bacteria
  • Chemical filtration removes dissolved compounds

Filter Types

  • Canister filters
  • Hang-on-back filters
  • Sponge filters
  • Internal filters

Filter Maintenance

Filter media should be rinsed in aquarium water to avoid killing beneficial bacteria that support the nitrogen cycle.
