Introduction to Aquariums

Aquariums recreate aquatic ecosystems inside a glass tank. Fish, plants, and beneficial bacteria work together to create a balanced environment.

Understanding water chemistry, filtration, species compatibility, and maintenance is essential to keeping fish healthy.
