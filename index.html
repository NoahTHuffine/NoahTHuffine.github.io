Introduction to Aquariums

Aquariums recreate aquatic ecosystems in a controlled environment. Fish, plants, microorganisms, and beneficial bacteria interact to create a balanced system.

This website explains the nitrogen cycle, filtration, tank planning, species compatibility, equipment, and how to solve common aquarium problems.
