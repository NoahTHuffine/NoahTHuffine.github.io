Introduction to Aquariums

Aquariums are small ecosystems that contain fish, plants, and beneficial bacteria. Maintaining a healthy aquarium requires proper filtration, water quality management, and compatible species.

This website will teach you about the nitrogen cycle, aquarium filtration, tank planning, species compatibility, equipment, and common aquarium problems.
