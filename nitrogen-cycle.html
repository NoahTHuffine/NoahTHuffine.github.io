Nitrogen Cycle

Fish waste produces ammonia, which is toxic. Beneficial bacteria convert ammonia into nitrite, and then another bacteria converts nitrite into nitrate.

Ammonia → Nitrite → Nitrate

Why Cycling a Tank Matters

Before adding fish, the aquarium must develop beneficial bacteria. This process is called cycling and usually takes several weeks.

Testing Water

  • pH
  • Ammonia
  • Nitrite
  • Nitrate
  • Temperature
  • Water hardness

Troubleshooting Water Quality

  • Ammonia spikes → reduce feeding
  • High nitrates → perform water changes
  • pH instability → check substrate and decorations
