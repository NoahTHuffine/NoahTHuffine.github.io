Nitrogen Cycle & Water Quality

The Nitrogen Cycle

Fish waste produces ammonia, which is toxic. Beneficial bacteria convert ammonia into nitrite, and another type of bacteria converts nitrite into nitrate.

Ammonia → Nitrite → Nitrate

Tank Cycling

Before adding fish, an aquarium must develop beneficial bacteria. This process usually takes several weeks.
