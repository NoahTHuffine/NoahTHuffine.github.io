Planning Your Aquarium

Tank Size

Larger tanks are generally easier to maintain because water conditions change more slowly.

Substrate Options

  • Gravel
  • Sand
  • Aquarium soil

Decorations

Rocks, driftwood, and caves provide hiding places for fish and help reduce stress.

Plants

  • Live plants improve water quality
  • Artificial plants require less maintenance
